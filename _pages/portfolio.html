Overview

My current research focuses on developing multiscale simulation methodologies for molecular beam experiments, encompassing a wide range of Knudsen numbers from continuum to rarefied flow regimes. In the continuum regime, I employ a finite volume approach to solve the Navier-Stokes equations, while in the rarefied regime, I utilize the Direct Simulation Monte Carlo (DSMC) approach to solve the Boltzmann equation. Additionally, my work delves into the interaction between nano-particles and gas flow. I am currently optimizing this simulation framework using data science methods.
